Columbia Neighborhood Dashboard

A no frills hub for mapping community resources and other useful links

Columbia Neighbors, click the image link the the right to open the map.We want to help you stay connected and up to date with what is happening in the world around you. To do this we have created a map where you can see local services and businesses that are still operating, and in what capacity.

How it works: If you have information to add to the map, please scroll down to the bottom of the page and submit a request to add information. A local captain will review the submission and add it to the map ASAP. This way our friends and neighbors can all be in the know in this time of need.

This is a community project if you have ideas of how to make this better, or just want to help email: columbianeighborhoodmap@gmail.com
